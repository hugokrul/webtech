Characters

Hiccup

Main Character

Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III is the protagonist of the movie. He is 15 years old and lives in Berk. His character is described by the Dreamworks fandom as witty, sarcastic, well-spoken but shy.

After his encounter with toothless, the rarest and most powerfull dragon known, he discovers his talent for riding and training dragons. and restores the trust the village has in him. Hiccup is voiced by Jay Baruchel.

Toothless

Dragon

Toothless is a Night Fury, one of the most rare dragons in the movie. He has cat like features and is dark black to help him blend in with the night sky.

The dragon ends up to be Hiccups best friend after Hiccup shoots him down to prove his place in the village. Hiccup takes care of toothless and helps heal the damages to make Toothless fly again, eventually bringing peace among dragons and humans. Toothless' sounds are designed by Randy Thom.

Astrid

(Girl)friend of hiccup

Astrid Hofferson-Haddock grow closer during the movie. First they Astrid shows no interest in Hiccup but during the movie the romance grows. With a kiss Astrid eventually does show romantic interest in Hiccup.

Astrid is very competitive hard working girl with Deadly Nadder named

Stoick

Dad of Hiccup

Stoick the Vast is the harsh dad of Hiccup. His wife Valka was taken by dragons so he raised Hiccup alone. Throughout the movie, Stoick becomes more supportive of Hiccup.

Stoick is the leader of the village Berk and before Hiccup shows dragons are not bad, Stoick has a passion for killing dragons. Stoick is voiced by Gerard Butler.

Fishlegs

Friend of hiccup

Fishlegs Ingerman is one of Hiccup's closest friends. He is clumsy and inept, always making mistakes at inappropriate moments. Stoick the Vast calls him "Fisheggs", and Snotface Snotlout and Dogsbreath pick on him constantly. In the first film and the three specials he is 14 years old. Fishlegs is voiced by Christopher Mintz-Plasse.

Snotlout

Friend of hiccup

Snotlout Jorgenson, Snotface Snotlout in the books, is a character from How to Train Your Dragon, both the book and films, in the books he is deceased while in the movies he's alive. He is 15 years old in the first film, 20 years old in the sequel, and 21 years old in the threequel. Snotlout is voice by Jonah Hill.

Gobber

Advisor of Stoick the Vast

Gobber The Belch is a veteran warrior of the Hairy Hooligans Tribe and a long-time friend and adviser of Stoick the Vast. He is 50 years old in the first film, and 55 in the sequel. He's a hefty Viking, and only has one leg (the other leg being replaced with a peg), but he can move much faster than you'd think. Gobber is voiced by Craig Ferguson.
