Characters

Hiccup

Main Character

Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III is the protagonist of the movie. He is 15 years old and lives in Berk. His character is described by the Dreamworks fandom as witty, sarcastic, well-spoken but shy.

After his encounter with Toothless, the rarest and most powerful dragon known, he discovers his talent for riding and training dragons. and restores the trust the village has in him. Hiccup is voiced by Jay Baruchel.

Toothless

Dragon

Toothless is a Night Fury, one of the most rare dragons in the movie. He has cat like features and is black as night to help him blend in with the dark skies.

The dragon ends up to be Hiccups best friend after Hiccup shoots him down to prove his place in the village. Hiccup takes care of Toothless and helps heal his wounds to make Toothless fly again, eventually bringing peace among dragons and humans. Toothless' sounds are designed by Randy Thom.

Astrid

(Girl)friend of Hiccup

Astrid Hofferson-Haddock grows closerbto Hiccup during the movie. In the beginning Astrid shows no interest in Hiccup but during the movie their romance grows. With a kiss Astrid eventually shows romantic interest in Hiccup.

Astrid is a very competitive and hard working girl with a Deadly Nadder named Stormfly as her dragon. For more information visit the Dreamworks fandom. Astrid is voiced by America Ferrera.

Stoick

Dad of Hiccup

Stoick the Vast is the harsh dad of Hiccup. His wife Valka was taken by dragons so he raised Hiccup alone. Throughout the movie, Stoick becomes more supportive of Hiccup.

Stoick is the leader of the village Berk and before Hiccup shows dragons are not bad, Stoick has a passion for killing dragons. Stoick is voiced by Gerard Butler.

Fishlegs

Friend of hiccup

Fishlegs Ingerman is one of Hiccup's closest friends. He is clumsy and inept, always making mistakes at inappropriate moments. Stoick the Vast calls him "Fisheggs", and Snotlout picks on him constantly. In the first film and the three specials he is 14 years old. Fishlegs is voiced by Christopher Mintz-Plasse.

Snotlout

Friend of hiccup

Snotlout Jorgenson, Snotface Snotlout in the books, is a character from How to Train Your Dragon, both in the book and films. In the books he is deceased while he's alive in the movies. He is 15 years old in the first film, 20 years old in the sequel, and 21 years old in the threequel. Snotlout is voiced by Jonah Hill.

Gobber

Advisor of Stoick the Vast

Gobber The Belch is a veteran warrior of the Hairy Hooligans Tribe and a long-time friend and adviser of Stoick the Vast. He is 50 years old in the first film, and 55 in the sequel. He's a hefty Viking, and only has one leg (the other leg being replaced with a peg), but he can move much faster than you'd think. Gobber is voiced by Craig Ferguson.


Side Characters

Tuffnut

Side Character

Tuffnut LaVerne Thorston is a member of the Hairy Hooligan Tribe. He is the fraternal twin of Ruffnut Thorston in the movie franchise and they are constantly arguing with each other, often to the point of physical violence though usually never seriously enough to separate them for too long. In the first film, the first television series, and in the shorts, he's 14 years old.

Ruffnut

Side character

Ruffnut Eugene Thorston is a member of the Hairy Hooligan Tribe. She is the fraternal twin of Tuffnut Thorston, whom she often argues with, sometimes to the point of physical violence, though never seriously enough to separate them for too long. She is 14 years old in the first film, the first three shorts, and first television series.

Ack

Side Character

Ack is seen partaking in the First Dragon War, defending Berk against the marauding dragons. He sees a young Hiccup trip outside during an attack when he should be inside, and wishes him a good morning. Despite this scene shows Ack actively taking his part in defending the Village, he is seen again just a few moments later standing on a nearby bridge, sporting a quite negligent attitude, picking his ear, while the others continue battling the dragons.

Hoark

Side character

During a Dragon Raid in the beginning of How to Train Your Dragon, Hoark the Haggard is one of the Vikings who yells at Hiccup to get back inside. Starkard later mentions Hoark when he reported to Chief Stoick what dragon species they were seeing during the raid. Hoark later was seen congratulating Stoick for Hiccup's success in Dragon Fighting, along with Ack, Starkard and Phlegma the Fierce.

Phlegma

Side Character

Phlegma the Fierce is a courageous Viking who has recently taken on the new position of Botanist at the School of Dragons. Having always had an especially green thumb, Phlegma is excited for the opportunity to teach more young Vikings about the natural world. She aspires to raise a new generation of students who respect and understand nature.

Spitelout

Side character

Spitelout Jorgenson is the father of Snotlout Jorgenson, the chief's second-in-command, and a warrior of the Hairy Hooligan Tribe. His dragons are a Deadly Nadder named Kingstail and an unnamed Snafflefang. He first appeared in How to Train Your Dragon.
