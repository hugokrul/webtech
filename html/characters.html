Characters

Hiccup

Main Character

Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III is the protagonist of the movie. He is 15 years old and lives in Berk. His character is described by dreamworks fandom as witty, sarcastic, well-spoken but shy.

After his encounter with toothless, the rarest and most powerfull dragon known, he discovers his talent for riding and training dragons.

Toothless

Dragon

Toothless is a Night Furry, one of the most rare dragons in the movie. He has cat like features and is dark black to help him blend in with the night sky.

The dragon ends up to be Hiccups best friend after Hiccup shoots him down to prove his place in the village. Hiccup takes care of toothless and helps heal the damages to make Toothless fly again, eventually bringing peace among dragons and humans.
